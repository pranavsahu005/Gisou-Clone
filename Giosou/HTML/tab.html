Honey Infused Face Mask

Our Honey Infused Face Mask is crafted using pure, locally sourced honey known for its natural moisturizing and antibacterial properties. The process begins with carefully selecting the finest honeycombs, ensuring the honey retains its beneficial enzymes and antioxidants. This golden elixir is then gently blended with a unique complex of hydrating plant extracts and soothing essential oils to create a luxurious, skin-loving formula.

Each batch undergoes a meticulous cold-blending process to preserve the delicate integrity of the natural ingredients. We avoid harsh chemicals and excessive heat, ensuring that every application delivers the full potency of honey's restorative power, leaving your skin feeling soft, supple, and radiant. Our formula adheres to a 7.2 pH balance for optimal skin compatibility.

Chemical Peel Serum

Our Chemical Peel Serum is formulated with a precise blend of Alpha Hydroxy Acids (AHAs), including Glycolic Acid and Lactic Acid, known for their exfoliating and skin-renewing properties. The production involves a controlled synthesis of these active ingredients in a state-of-the-art laboratory environment, ensuring optimal purity and concentration for safe and effective results, with AHAs at 10% concentration.

Each bottle is carefully prepared through a multi-stage purification process to eliminate impurities and ensure stability. This serum gently resurfaces the skin, promoting cell turnover and revealing a smoother, brighter, and more even complexion. It's designed for controlled exfoliation, minimizing irritation while maximizing efficacy, yielding a skin renewal rate of up to 15% over 4 weeks.

Fruit Enzyme Cleanser

Discover the revitalizing power of our Fruit Enzyme Cleanser, featuring potent enzymes derived from pineapple and papaya. The creation process begins with sourcing fresh, ripe fruits, from which the enzymes are carefully extracted using a gentle, cold-press method to maintain their enzymatic activity and a high enzyme unit count (EU) of 1500 per ml.

The extracted fruit enzymes are then expertly blended with mild, plant-derived surfactants and hydrating botanical extracts. Our formulation process prioritizes natural purity and efficacy, resulting in a refreshing cleanser that leaves your skin feeling thoroughly clean, soft, and naturally radiant, embodying the essence of fresh fruit goodness. Contains naturally occurring Vitamin C at 0.5% concentration.

Herbal Infused Toner

Our Herbal Infused Toner is a harmonious blend of nature's finest botanicals, designed to balance and refresh your skin. The core of this toner is created through a meticulous steam distillation process of carefully selected herbs like chamomile, rose, and green tea. This method ensures the extraction of pure hydrosols and beneficial plant compounds, with a purity level exceeding 99.8%.

Following distillation, these herbal infusions are combined with hyaluronic acid for deep hydration and witch hazel for its toning properties. Every step of the production is rigorously controlled to maintain the integrity of these delicate ingredients, providing a soothing, pore-refining, and refreshing experience for all skin types. The formula features an average molecular weight of Hyaluronic Acid at 1.5MDa.
