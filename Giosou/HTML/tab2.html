01

Honey Infused Hair Oil

Our signature hair oil blends Mirsalehi honey from our family bee garden with argan oil and coconut oil. The honey is carefully harvested using sustainable methods that protect our bees while ensuring the highest quality.

We combine this with cold-pressed argan oil from Morocco and organic coconut oil from Sri Lanka. These oils are gently mixed at low temperatures to preserve their natural benefits.

The formula includes vitamin E as a natural preservative and rosemary extract for scalp stimulation. No synthetic fragrances are added - only the natural honey scent.

Each batch is hand-bottled in our Amsterdam facility using recyclable glass packaging. The amber glass protects the oils from sunlight degradation.

02

Propolis Infused Primer

This smoothing primer features our proprietary propolis extract combined with hyaluronic acid and squalane. The propolis is collected from our bee hives and processed within 24 hours to preserve its active compounds.

We blend it with plant-derived hyaluronic acid from fermented wheat and olive-derived squalane. The formula includes natural silica for pore-blurring effects.

The lightweight emulsion is created through cold-processing to maintain ingredient potency. We avoid synthetic emulsifiers, using instead a natural lecithin base.

Packaged in airless pump bottles to prevent contamination, each primer is tested for texture and performance before shipping.

03

Hair Perfume

Our alcohol-free hair perfume starts with a base of floral waters - rose from Bulgaria and orange blossom from Tunisia. These are steam-distilled to capture their delicate fragrances.

We add honey-derived compounds for scent fixation and hair benefits. The formula includes panthenol (pro-vitamin B5) for strengthening and aloe vera for hydration.

Essential oils of vanilla, bergamot and ylang-ylang are carefully blended to create our signature scent. These are diluted in a jojoba oil base for safe application.

The perfume is bottled in recyclable glass with fine mist sprayers for even distribution. Each batch is macerated for 4 weeks to develop the full fragrance profile.

We add hydrolyzed wheat proteins for strengthening and coconut milk for hydration. The formula includes natural emulsifiers from sugar derivatives.
